Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the total investment required for a Mr. Golisoda franchise? +

The total investment for a Mr. Golisoda manufacturing franchise is ₹45 Lakhs + GST. This covers machinery, equipment, software support, and initial launch assistance.

What kind of returns can I expect from the franchise? +

We offer a guaranteed monthly return of ₹1.75 Lakhs for a period of 2.5 years, ensuring a stable income and quick return on your investment.

What kind of area and resources are required for the factory setup? +

You will need approximately 1000 sq ft area for the factory setup. Additionally, you will need to arrange for one supply vehicle for distribution.

Do you provide training and support for new franchise owners? +

Yes, we provide comprehensive A-Z training for your manpower, along with dedicated software support for operations, inventory, and sales management. Our launch team also assists in establishing your market presence.

How long has Mr. Golisoda been in business? +

Mr. Golisoda has a proven track record of 8 successful years in the market, establishing itself as a leading traditional soda brand in India.
